About Us

Baba Vasudev Public School provides a balanced curriculum that emphasizes academic excellence, moral values and extracurricular growth. Located in Naina Jhala, we serve the Sant Kabir Nagar community with modern facilities and an experienced faculty.

Mission

To empower every student to achieve their full potential through quality education, discipline and service to society.

Vision

To be a centre of excellence, producing compassionate leaders and lifelong learners.
